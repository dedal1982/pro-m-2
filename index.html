THE BEST TOURS

Here are the best tours selected by our specialists especially for you!

Duration : 10 days

Cultural Explorations of Europe

Embark on a 10-day journey through the captivating cities of Europe, starting with the romantic streets of Paris, where you can marvel at the Eiffel Tower and wander through the charming Montmartre district. Indulge in delicious French cuisine, from croissants and macarons to escargot and coq au vin, and sip on fine wines in the vineyards of Bordeaux. Continue your adventure to the ancient city of Rome, where you can explore the Colosseum, Vatican City, and the Trevi Fountain. Sample authentic Italian pasta, pizza, and gelato, and soak up the vibrant atmosphere of the bustling piazzas. Conclude your tour in the vibrant city of Barcelona, known for its unique architecture by Gaudi, such as the Sagrada Familia and Park Guell. Experience the lively tapas culture, with a variety of small dishes to enjoy along with sangria or cava. Immerse yourself in the artistic spirit of Barcelona, with its museums, galleries, and street art scene, and discover the rich history and vibrant culture of Catalonia. Explore the Gothic Quarter, stroll along the famous Las Ramblas, and relax on the sandy beaches of the Mediterranean. Capture the essence of European charm and beauty as you journey through these iconic cities and create memories to last a lifetime.

Duration : 14 days

Adventure in the Amazon Rainforest

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure as you delve into the heart of the Amazon rainforest, one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth. Explore lush greenery and towering trees as you hike through the dense jungles, listening to the symphony of exotic bird calls and rustling leaves. Encounter indigenous tribes with rich cultural traditions and learn about their way of life, deep-rooted connection to nature, and ancient rituals. Keep your eyes peeled for elusive jaguars, colorful macaws, playful monkeys, and the elusive giant river otters as you navigate through the wilderness. Immerse yourself in the sounds and scents of the rainforest, from the earthy aroma of damp soil to the chorus of insects buzzing in the humid air. Marvel at the breathtaking beauty of cascading waterfalls, crystal-clear rivers, and vibrant tropical flowers that dot the landscape. Experience the thrill of a night safari, where you can witness nocturnal creatures like tree frogs, snakes, and spiders emerging under the cover of darkness. Relax by the tranquil waters of the Amazon River, taking in the stunning sunsets and listening to the soothing sounds of the flowing water. As you journey deeper into this wild and untouched paradise, you'll gain a new appreciation for the delicate balance of life in this extraordinary ecosystem.Capture the essence of the Amazon's raw, untamed beauty and take home memories of an incredible and life-changing expedition.

Duration : 10 days

Safari Adventure in Africa

Prepare to be awe-struck as you embark on an exhilarating safari adventure in Africa, a land of immense beauty and diverse wildlife. Witness the majestic Big Five - lions, elephants, buffaloes, leopards, and rhinoceroses - up close in their natural habitat, creating memories that will last a lifetime. Enjoy the thrill of game drives through the vast African savannah, where every turn brings a new and exciting wildlife encounter. Immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of the bush as you listen to the roar of lions, the trumpeting of elephants, and the chatter of monkeys in the distance. Indulge in traditional bush dinners under the star-studded African sky, surrounded by the flickering glow of campfires and the calls of nocturnal animals in the distance. Experience the magic of waking up to a sunrise over the savannah, painting the landscape in hues of pink and orange as the animals begin their day. Capture stunning photographs of giraffes gracefully grazing, zebras trotting across the plains, and cheetahs lounging lazily in the shade of acacia trees. Feel the adrenaline rush as you track elusive leopards through the dense bush, following their paw prints and scent trails with experienced guides. Immerse yourself in the rich culture of the local tribes, learning about their traditions, dances, and way of life that have been passed down for generations. At the end of your safari adventure, take a moment to reflect on the profound beauty and raw power of Africa's wilderness, leaving you with a deep appreciation for the natural world and a longing to return to this enchanting land.

Duration : 8 days

Historical Treasures of Egypt

Embark on a journey through time as you explore the ancient wonders of Egypt, a land steeped in history and mythology. Stand in awe before the iconic Pyramids of Giza, marveling at the architectural brilliance of the ancient Egyptians and the mysteries that shroud these monumental structures. Cruise along the legendary Nile River, the lifeblood of Egypt, and witness daily life unfolding on its banks as you pass by traditional villages and bustling markets. Step back in time as you wander through the halls of the Egyptian Museum, home to a wealth of artifacts and treasures, including the legendary mask of Tutankhamun, giving you a glimpse into the opulence and grandeur of Egypt's pharaohs. Delve into the intricate myths and legends of ancient Egypt, from the powerful gods and goddesses to the intricate burial practices that ensured a prosperous afterlife for the deceased. Feel the magic of Egypt's history come alive as you explore the temples of Luxor and Karnak, where colossal statues and intricate carvings tell the stories of a civilization that flourished thousands of years ago. Let yourself be transported to a world of wonder and mystery as you uncover the secrets of Egypt's past, leaving you with a profound appreciation for the enduring legacy of this remarkable civilization.

Duration : 8 days

Island Hopping in Greece

Discover the charm and beauty of the Greek islands as you wander through picturesque villages with their whitewashed buildings and narrow cobblestone streets, immersing yourself in the timeless allure of this idyllic destination. Feel the warm golden sand between your toes as you relax on the pristine beaches that fringe the crystal-clear waters of the Aegean Sea, offering a tranquil oasis for sun-seekers and water sports enthusiasts alike. Dive into the rich history of Greece as you explore the ancient ruins and archaeological sites that tell the story of millennia-old civilizations, from the majestic temples of Athens to the sacred sites of Delphi and Olympia. Indulge your senses in the flavors of the Mediterranean as you savor the delights of Greek cuisine, with its fresh seafood, succulent olives, and aromatic herbs infusing each dish with a taste of tradition and local flavor. Cruise along the azure waters of the Aegean, admiring the breathtaking scenery of rugged cliffs, hidden coves, and lush landscapes that characterize these enchanting islands, beckoning you to explore their hidden treasures. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and traditions of the Greek islands, where music, dance, and folklore come alive in lively festivals and celebrations that showcase the spirit of this proud nation. Let the magic of the Greek islands captivate your heart and soul, leaving you with memories of a journey filled with beauty, history, and unforgettable experiences that will stay with you long after you've returned home.
